Nedra Cullen-O'Brien

Nedra Cullen-O'Brien

Studying Creative Digital Media at ITB, Nedra is pursuing a career in the Film Industry. Most interested in film-making from a young age, has made many short films and taken part in many film and dramatic productions, with addition of experience in radio. Having fluency in the Irish language, has also taken part in film-making through Irish. Nedra is most at home editing and participating in the production of infomertials, music videos and short films, and hopes to expand that to the big screen in the future.
Having a great interest in human rights, mental health and the wellbeing of others, is constantly seeking ways to help improve her community. Active within her community in both charity and voulenteer work and also as a recipient of the President’s award. She was a Student Mentor, Acted as Student Council Chairperson and served on the Student Council all throughout secondary school. Having been elected to Comhairle na NÓg, was very active in improving life in the community for young people, working closely with the County Council and Mental Health Associations such as Mindspace. Committed and enthusiastic about her work, she puts passion and effort into all she sets her mind to.
